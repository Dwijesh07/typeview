OUR STORY

A Taste of Italy in Every Bite

Founded in 2012 by the Rossi family, La Terrazza brings generations of Italian culinary tradition to the heart of the city. Every recipe has been passed down through three generations, perfected over time.

We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients combined with authentic Italian imports to create dishes that transport you to the hills of Tuscany.

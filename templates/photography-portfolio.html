About The Artist

James Carter

James Carter is a fine art and portrait photographer based in New York City. His work explores the interplay between light and shadow, capturing the quiet beauty of everyday moments.

With over a decade of experience, James's photographs have been exhibited internationally and featured in publications such as Vogue Italia, Harper's Bazaar, and The New Yorker.

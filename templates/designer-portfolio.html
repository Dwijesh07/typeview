About

Sophia Lin

Sophia Lin is a multidisciplinary designer specializing in brand identity, digital products, and creative direction. Her work bridges the gap between minimalism and emotion, creating designs that resonate deeply with audiences.

With over 8 years of experience collaborating with startups, agencies, and global brands, Sophia brings a unique perspective to every project — one that prioritizes authenticity, clarity, and human connection.
